The Latrobe University Botanical Gardens

Step into a world of natural beauty and tranquility at the Latrobe University Botanical Gardens, a hidden oasis nestled within the heart of the university campus. This lush and diverse green space is a sanctuary for both relaxation and exploration, offering a connection to nature that enhances the university experience.

The Latrobe University Botanical Gardens provide a respite from the hustle and bustle of academic life. Whether you seek solace in nature, wish to expand your knowledge of plant life, or simply desire a scenic escape, our gardens are a gateway to the beauty and wonder of the natural world.

Join us at the Latrobe University Botanical Gardens and immerse yourself in the serenity and splendor of nature right in the heart of the campus.
