Welcome to the InfoSysAnalysis AGM at La Trobe University!

We are thrilled to extend our warmest greetings to all delegates, attendees, and guests. It's our pleasure to invite you to the Annual General Meeting of the Victorian Branch of InfoSysAnalysis, hosted on the picturesque grounds of La Trobe University.

This AGM is not just a meeting; it's an opportunity to unite, exchange ideas, and propel our collective knowledge to new heights. We're dedicated to making this event both informative and enjoyable.

Our website is your gateway to all the information you need for the AGM. Getting to La Trobe University

The Melbourne (Bundoora) campus of La Trobe University is one of its largest and most well-known campuses. Here are directions to the Melbourne campus:

La Trobe University Melbourne (Bundoora) Campus

Address: Kingsbury Dr, Bundoora VIC 3086, Australia

By Public Transportation:

  1. Train: From Melbourne's city center, you can catch a train on the South Morang line to the "Reservoir" or "MacLeod" station. These stations are the closest to the Melbourne campus.
  2. Bus: Once you arrive at Reservoir or MacLeod train station, you can take a bus to the La Trobe University Melbourne campus. There are various bus services that connect to the campus.

By Car:

  1. If you're driving, you can use GPS navigation or online maps to navigate to the Melbourne campus. The campus is located in the suburb of Bundoora, which is northeast of Melbourne's city center.
  2. There are multiple entry points to the campus, so follow the signs once you get close to the university.

Parking Information

La Trobe University offers ample parking facilities for attendees. Please follow these guidelines for parking during the AGM:

  • On-Campus Parking: There are designated parking areas on campus. Please refer to the campus map for the location of these parking lots. Parking permits will be provided upon registration at the AGM.
  • Accessibility: If you require accessible parking spaces, please let us know in advance, and we will make the necessary arrangements to ensure your convenience.
  • Public Transportation: If you prefer to use public transportation, there are drop-off zones and convenient access to campus buildings.
