The Latrobe University Modern Art Gallery

Immerse yourself in a world of creativity, innovation, and self-expression at the Latrobe University Modern Art Gallery. Nestled within the heart of the university campus, this dynamic art space is a hub for contemporary artists and enthusiasts, offering a transformative experience in the realm of modern art.

The Latrobe University Modern Art Gallery continuously showcases avant-garde exhibitions that push the boundaries of contemporary art. Explore a diverse range of mediums, from paintings and sculptures to multimedia installations and interactive displays.

The gallery is known for its dedication to experimental art forms. It features dedicated areas for interactive and immersive experiences, allowing visitors to engage with art on a deeper level.

The Latrobe University Local Art Gallery

Immerse yourself in the vibrant world of local art at the Latrobe University Local Art Space, a hidden gem within the university's campus. This intimate gallery space celebrates the creative talents of students, faculty, and the wider community, offering a canvas for emerging and established artists to showcase their work.

The Local Art Space hosts a diverse array of exhibitions that highlight various art forms, from paintings and photography to mixed media and sculpture. These exhibitions are carefully curated to reflect the rich tapestry of artistic expression in the local community.

Discover a world where creativity thrives and art becomes a bridge between the university and the local community. The Latrobe University Art Galleries, including the Local Art Space and the Modern Art Gallery, are celebrations of local talent and artistic exploration. Whether you're an artist, an art enthusiast, a student seeking inspiration, or a visitor in search of a unique cultural experience, our galleries are welcoming spaces where you can connect with the ever-evolving world of art in all its forms.

Join us at the Latrobe University Art Galleries, where innovation, imagination, and the beauty of local artistry converge to create vibrant, thought-provoking spaces that celebrate contemporary art and the talents of our community.
